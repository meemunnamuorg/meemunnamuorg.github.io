Sri. Gandham Mallikarjuna Rao Founder

Welcome to Meemunnamu Welfare Society

Meemunnamu Welfare Society is a registered NGO founded by Late Sri. Gandham Mallikarjuna Rao garu, Govt. Teacher in Telangana. Mallikarjun sir used to help poor students throughout his career. Early 2010’s, Sir had a thought of starting an organization works for poor & talented students, improving health awareness in public, improving social responsibility towards society and ultimately a peaceful world.

Shared his thoughts with his students and inspired them to start an organization works primarily for Health, Peace, Wisdom, and Environment. Finally in 2015, his favorite student Bonepalli Narsimha Rao as president, the organization has begun its journey. Around 10 members of sir’s students started the organizational activities since then. Despite the demise of Mallikarjun sir in 2017, organization is finding its responsibilities towards the society and working. And now, not only sir’s students but also many young students joined their hands for the activities happening in the organization.
