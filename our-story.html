Sri. Gandham Mallikarjuna Rao Founder

Meemunnamu Welfare Society is a registered NGO founded by Late Sri. Gandham Mallikarjuna Rao garu, Govt. Teacher in Telangana. Mallikarjun sir used to help poor students throughout his career. Early 2010’s, he thought of starting an organization that works for financially backward and talented students. Which also focuses on improving health and environmental awareness, improving social responsibility towards society, and ultimately a peaceful world.

Shared his thoughts with his favorite student Dr. Narasimha Rao Bonepally and inspired him to lead an organization works primarily for Health, Peace, Wisdom, and Environment. Finally, in 2015 Dr. Narsimha Rao as president, the organization began its journey. Around ten members of his students started the organizational activities since then.

Despite the demise of Mallikarjun sir in 2017, our organization is finding its responsibilities towards society and working. And now, not only his students also many young students and professionals joined their hands for the activities happening in the organization.
