RALLY AGAINST ROAD ACCIDENTS

Meemunnamu have made a rally against road accidents in the year of 2015. We all know how road accidents have been happening in the country for past some years. The reasons are many. Even our organization lost some of our valuable members (D. Sreenivasa rao garu, Sk. John Pasha garu) in road accidents. Many people in Wyra met with accidents and lost their lives and left families alone.

We made A research said, deaths in road accidents in our country is the second largest death causes in unnatural deaths. We thought of doing an awareness program and decided to conduct a walk from Wyra to Khammam with almost 50-70 members.

Our With the great support of wyra people, we made it possible on 28th December 2015. Sub Inspector of Wyra police, Sri. Srinivas garu started the rally at Wyra police station. Shabari Waters Jagan garu, smt D.Sireesha (Lawyer by profession), have participated and gave their message. Khammam traffic Inspector have recieved at the bus stand and spoke with the media. Sri. Gandham Mallikarjuna Rao garu and Bollakonda Satyanarayana garu lead the entire team from Wyra to Khammam and made it a successful event.
