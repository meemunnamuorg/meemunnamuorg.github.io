Programs

Health

We organize Medical, Blood Donation camps, Awareness about Mental health. Gradually conduct Seminars, Oath-taking programs, signing for Organ donation in the future.

Peace

We encourage people to find peaceful solutions to problems in society and inculcate spiritual values from a scientific perspective. We also conduct safety awareness programs.

Wisdom

We provide financial assistance to poor meritorious students and conduct Skill development programs. Inspire students to develop Social responsibility and Leadership skills.

Environment

We conduct Plantation drives. Gradually conduct awareness about reducing Plastic usage, saving Natural resources, usage of Alternative energy, and Rainwater harvesting.

Latest Events

10 th
May
Blood Donation Camp

Blood scarcity increased for thalassemia and people with blood disorders as people are not allowed to come out due to lockdown. We organized a blood camp with utmost care, 73 kind donors came forward and donated blood.

3rd
May
Groceries Distribution

Due to COVID-19 pandemic poor people are suffering to get food because of lockdown. We distributed groceries and vegetables to 700+ needy families in Khammam district. Disributed Masks and Sanitizers to Healthcare professionals.

08 th
Apr
COVID-19 relief fund to CMRF

We believe Telangana Govt. doing well and responding sharply in the process of fighting COVID-19 pandemic. We raised 30,501 rupees for CMRF and presented cheque to our local MLA Sri. Lavudya Ramulu Nayak.
