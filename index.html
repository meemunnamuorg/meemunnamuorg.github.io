Our Programs

Education

One of our primary goals is, no student should stop education because of financial problems. At the same time, student should not compromise on quality of education.

Talent hunt & Encouragement

Essay writing, Elocution, Debate competitions. As a team we have been conducting competitions in Govt. Schools and Junior colleges from 2017.


Health Awareness

Awarness about blood donation. As we working in second class cities, towns we gradually make seminars, oath taking programs, signing organ donating agreements in future.

Environment & Peace

Plantation drives for various govt. schools awareness on avoiding plastic. Conducting more awareness sessions for peace.
